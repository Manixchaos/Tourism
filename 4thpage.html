VISIT SAUDI ARABIA

TRAVEL TIPS

Planning your trip to Saudi Arabia? Here is everything you need to know before you go.

Visa Information

Saudi Arabia offers tourist e-visas to citizens of over 60 countries. Apply online through the official Visitsaudi.com portal. Visas are valid for 1 year with multiple entries of up to 90 days each.

Best Time to Visit

October through March offers the most comfortable weather (15–25°C). Summer months (June–August) are extremely hot in most regions, except the Asir Mountains where temperatures remain mild.

Currency & Payments

The Saudi Riyal (SAR) is the official currency. Cards are widely accepted in cities. ATMs are plentiful. It is recommended to carry some cash for markets and rural areas.

Getting Around

Riyadh and Jeddah have modern metro systems. Ride-hailing apps (Uber, Careem) are widely available. Renting a car is ideal for exploring regions like AlUla, Tabuk, and the Asir Mountains.

Dress Code

Modest clothing is recommended for all visitors. Women are not required to wear an abaya in public, but covering shoulders and knees is respectful and advised, especially at religious sites.

Language & Communication

Arabic is the official language. English is widely spoken in hotels, malls, and tourist areas. Download an offline Arabic phrasebook app for more remote destinations.
