Hail Region

Home to the Jubbah and Shuwaymis rock art sites — ancient petroglyphs inscribed by early humans and listed as UNESCO World Heritage. A must-see for history enthusiasts.

Hail Region's History

Located in north-central Saudi Arabia, the Hail region is a historically significant area known as a prehistoric hub and a key stop on ancient caravan routes. Formerly the capital of the Rashidi dynasty (1836–1921), Hail served as a major center for Arabian culture, trade, and Bedouin traditions, featuring UNESCO-listed rock art and historical forts like Qishlash Palace.
