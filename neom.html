NEOM & The Line

A glimpse into the future — NEOM is Saudi Arabia's visionary megaproject on the Red Sea coast, featuring futuristic cities, luxury resorts, and the world's most ambitious urban design.

NEOM & The Line's History

NEOM is a $500-billion, 26,500 km² futuristic mega-project in Saudi Arabia’s Tabuk Province, launched by Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman in 2017 to diversify the economy beyond oil. As a cornerstone of Vision 2030, it focuses on sustainability, renewable energy, and technology. Key regions include The Line, Oxagon, and Trojena, with plans scaling back in 2026 amid rising costs.
