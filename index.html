VISIT SAUDI ARABIA

THE KINGDOM AWAITS

From ancient deserts to modern skylines, Saudi Arabia offers a journey unlike any other. Discover centuries of history, breathtaking landscapes, and legendary hospitality.

TOP ATTRACTIONS

AlUla

A vast open-air museum in northwest Saudi Arabia, home to Hegra — the Kingdom's first UNESCO World Heritage Site. Marvel at ancient Nabataean tombs carved into rose-red rock.

HERITAGE

Riyadh

The dynamic capital blends futuristic architecture with deep-rooted tradition. Visit the historic Diriyah district, the towering Kingdom Centre, and the National Museum.

CITY

Jeddah

A coastal gem on the Red Sea. Explore the UNESCO-listed Al-Balad old district, vibrant souks, and the world's tallest fountain — the King Fahd Fountain.

COASTAL

Rub' al Khali

The Empty Quarter — the world's largest continuous sand desert. A dramatic landscape of towering dunes, silence, and starlit skies that captivates every traveler.

NATURE

Tabuk Region

Home to pristine Red Sea dive sites, lush canyons of Wadi Disah, and the historic Hejaz Railway. A haven for outdoor adventurers and history lovers alike.

ADVENTURE

Abha

Nestled in the cool Asir Mountains, Abha offers a refreshing escape. Explore misty highlands, traditional villages, and the colorful culture of the Asir people.
